# Autonomy and Scheduler Audit

## Mapped outbound systems
- automation runner / scheduled actions
- heartbeat
- life runtime and alive engine/executor
- inner life self-check / interaction observation
- emotional arc scheduler
- proactive action store and follow-ups
- timed notes, romantic surprises, journals/dream writers

## Findings
- Outbound paths generally include source identifiers in logs/metadata, but full source attribution manifests are not centralized.
- Fire-and-forget `.catch(() => {})` is common; this prevents crashes but can hide repeated failures.

## Safe changes
- No autonomy cadence, quiet hours, kill switches, or outbound content behavior changed.
